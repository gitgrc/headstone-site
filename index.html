About Us

We are specialized in making custom designed headstones with Islamic prayers of your choice. Languages includes Arabic, English, Urdu and Bengali. We work closely with you to custom tailor the monument to your requirements.

Services

Home Visits

We know it can be difficult for some customers to get to us and we are more than happy to come and visit you at your home or work for a no obligation quote. Please contact us to make an appointment.

Bespoke

Our selection is not limited to items seen in our brochure and showroom. Should you want something even more unique, our specialists will design a beautiful and one-of-a-kind headstone that will be a lasting memory of your loved one.

Speedy Service

We have general lead-times but if you need a headstone or renovation completed before a specific date speak to us and we will do our utmost to help.
